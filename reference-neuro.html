Neurologic Emergencies

Neuro & Stroke

ICP control, seizure ladders, and cerebrovascular blood pressure windows packaged for rapid review.

Weight Input

Enter weight to convert mg/kg, mcg/kg, or g/kg therapies into ready-to-chart numbers.

Increased ICP

  • Mannitol 20%: 0.25–1 g/kg (→ --).
  • Hypertonic saline: 3 mL/kg.
  • Dexamethasone: 10 mg IV.

Seizure Rescue

  • Lorazepam: 2–4 mg IV.
  • Midazolam: 5–10 mg IM.
  • Diazepam: 5–10 mg IV.
  • Levetiracetam: 20–60 mg/kg IV (max 4.5 g) (→ --).
  • Phenytoin/fosphenytoin: 20 mg/kg (→ --).
  • Valproic acid: 20–30 mg/kg (→ --).

Status Epilepticus

  • Lorazepam: 4 mg IV, repeat as needed.
  • Midazolam infusion: 0.04–0.6 mg/kg/hr (→ --).
  • Propofol: Bolus 1–2 mg/kg (→ --) then infusion 5–80 mcg/kg/min (→ --).
  • Phenobarbital load: 20 mg/kg (→ --), transition to pentobarbital 0.5–5 mg/kg/hr (→ --).

Intracerebral Hemorrhage (IPH)

  • BP goal: SBP < 140 mmHg.
  • Nicardipine: Start 5 mg/hr, increase by 2.5 mg/hr q5–15 min (max 15 mg/hr).
  • Clevidipine: Start 1–2 mg/hr, double every 90 sec to goal (max 16 mg/hr).
  • Levetiracetam: 20–60 mg/kg IV (max 4.5 g) for seizure prophylaxis (→ --).

Cerebrovascular Accident

  • BP goals: <220 mmHg unless receiving thrombolytics; then <180 mmHg.
  • tPA (alteplase): Bolus 0.09 mg/kg (max 9 mg) (→ --) then 0.81 mg/kg over 60 min (max 81 mg) (→ --) — total 0.9 mg/kg (max 90 mg) (→ --).
  • tNK (tenecteplase): 0.25 mg/kg IV bolus (max 25 mg) (→ --).
