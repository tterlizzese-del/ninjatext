Non-Pregnant Vaginal Bleeding

  • TXA: 10 mg/kg IV (max 600 mg) over 10 min.
  • Conjugated estrogen (Premarin): 25 mg IV q4–6 h until bleeding stops.
  • Bakri balloon: Insert into uterus, instill 250 mL sterile water and increase up to 500 mL until hemostasis.
