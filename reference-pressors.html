Continuous Infusions

  • Norepinephrine: 0.01–1 mcg/kg/min (→ --).
  • Epinephrine: 0.01–1 mcg/kg/min (→ --).
  • Dobutamine: 2.5–20 mcg/kg/min (→ --).
  • Dopamine: 5–20 mcg/kg/min (→ --).
  • Vasopressin: 0.04 U/min (fixed dose).
  • Phenylephrine: 0.5–6 mcg/kg/min (→ --).

Dirty Epinephrine Drip

Use when you need a quick push-dose friendly drip before pharmacy bags arrive.

  • Inject 1 mg (1 mL) of crash-cart epinephrine into a 1 L bag of normal saline.
  • The resulting solution is 1 mcg/mL.
  • Run the line wide open and titrate to blood pressure response.
