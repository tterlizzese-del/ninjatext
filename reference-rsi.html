Rapid Sequence Intubation

Airway & RSI

Side-by-side induction, paralytic, and post-intubation sedation dosing to streamline medication planning.

Weight Input

Plug in patient weight to translate mg/kg or mcg/kg orders into real-world doses.

Induction Agents

  • Propofol: 1.5–2 mg/kg (→ --).
  • Ketamine: 1–2 mg/kg (→ --).
  • Etomidate: 0.3 mg/kg (→ --).
  • Midazolam: 0.3 mg/kg (→ --).

Paralytics

  • Rocuronium: 0.8–1.2 mg/kg (→ --).
  • Succinylcholine: 1–1.5 mg/kg (→ --).

Post-Intubation Sedation

  • Propofol: Bolus 0.5–1 mg/kg (→ --); infusion 5–80 mcg/kg/min (→ --).
  • Midazolam: Bolus 1–5 mg; infusion 0.04–0.2 mg/kg/hr (→ --).
  • Lorazepam: Bolus 1–4 mg; infusion 0.01–0.1 mg/kg/hr (→ --).
  • Dexmedetomidine: Infusion 0.2–1.5 mcg/kg/hr (→ --).
  • Fentanyl: Bolus 1–3 mcg/kg (→ --); infusion 25–250 mcg/hr.
  • Morphine: Bolus 0.5–10 mg; infusion 2–30 mg/hr.
