Endocrine/Renal Emergencies

Endocrine & Renal Cliff Notes

Hyponatremia rescue, hyperkalemia protection, thyroid storm, and myxedema coma therapies.

Hyponatremia

  • If unresponsive or seizing: 3% NaCl 100 mL over 10–20 min; repeat ×1 if still symptomatic.

Hyperkalemia

  • Calcium: 2 g calcium gluconate or 1 g calcium chloride IV, repeat as needed for membrane stabilization.
  • Insulin + Dextrose: 10 U IV regular insulin with D25/D50.
  • Albuterol: 10–20 mg nebulized.

Thyroid Storm

  • PTU: 500–1,000 mg PO load, then 250 mg PO q4h.
  • Hydrocortisone: 100 mg IV.

Myxedema Coma

  • Levothyroxine: 300 mcg IV.
  • Hydrocortisone: 100 mg IV.
