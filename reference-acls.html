Defibrillation Energy

Use these biphasic energy recommendations for pulseless VT/VF.

  • Initial shock: 120–200 J biphasic.
  • Subsequent shocks: Maintain 120–200 J (or manufacturer guidance) for repeat attempts.

Synchronized Cardioversion

Match the energy to the rhythm in front of you.

  • Narrow regular: 50–100 J.
  • Narrow irregular: 120–200 J biphasic.
  • Wide regular: 100 J.
