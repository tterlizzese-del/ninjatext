Instructions:

  1. Give 2-3 × IV dose through ET, except for Epinephrine (×10 IV dose).
  2. Dilute med in 5 mL NS.
  3. Hold compressions, give dilute med, follow with 5 mL flush.
  4. Deliver 5 breaths after the flush.
